I haven't posted in a while. Shame shame shame... Today I am writing to you about YouTube's live streaming service. I was checking out the Asp.Net weekly stand-up and noticed, it's what they use. I went off to investigate and realized it's really simple to setup.

Navigate off to https://www.youtube.com/live_dashboard and agree to the terms. You'll notice then that there is a "Stream Now" link. Click that...

Off on the right conveniently located near the video player is a checklist. This checklist walks you through setting up an encoder and hooking it up to YouTube.

I went with Wirecast, because I have a copy. I'd recommend downloading Wirecast Play free. It's a pretty awesome tool and will authenticate with your YouTube account. Then click stream...

As a person with close ties and invested interest in Azure Media Services, we need something this easy, this free, and this without the need for an Office365 subscription.

Maybe I'll setup a reoccurring live stream for us folks in the ATL... hmmm.
